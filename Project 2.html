Motion Detection Application

A Motion Detection Application for Self-Driving Cars is a critical software component that utilizes advanced computer vision and machine learning algorithms to detect, track, and respond to movements and changes in the vehicle's surroundings.

Overview

The Motion Detection Application is an integral software component designed for self-driving cars to identify and analyze movements and changes in the vehicle's surroundings. It employs advanced computer vision and machine learning algorithms to detect, track, and respond to dynamic objects, ensuring enhanced safety and navigation in real-time scenarios.

Key Features and Functionalities

  • Object Detection:

    • Utilizing computer vision algorithms to identify and categorize various objects such as pedestrians, vehicles, cyclists, and obstacles.

  • Motion Tracking:

    • Continuously tracking the movement and trajectories of detected objects to predict their future positions.

  • Collision Avoidance:

    • Implementing algorithms to calculate safe trajectories and assist the vehicle in avoiding collisions with detected obstacles or pedestrians.
